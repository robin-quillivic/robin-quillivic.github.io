Post-doctoral Researcher in Natural Language Processing

I am a post-doctoral researcher at CESSP/ISC-PIF, developing Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) for PTSD detection through Natural Language Processing. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from EPHE and lead the 150k€ CNRS-funded TALANT project on trauma language analysis.

Contact Me GitHub

About Me

I specialize in leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) to address mental health challenges, with a focus on identifying linguistic markers of trauma and PTSD. My interdisciplinary background bridges computer science, psycholinguistics, and clinical research. I am passionate about building explainable AI solutions that support clinicians and advance our understanding of human language.
